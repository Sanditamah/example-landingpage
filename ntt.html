EAST NUSA TENGGARA





EXPLORE EAST COAST OF LOMBOK

This three day journey will bring you to explore the sea and small islands on the east coast of Lombok. You will have opportunity to enjoy the hiking through monsoon forest to the waterfall on Moyo island, meet people who live on small island, fishing, swimming and snorkeling.



Duration: 3 days / 2 nights.
Participant: 2, 3-5, 6-8.
Type of accommodation: Hotel, Local people house.
Type of transportation: Minibus, Boat
Start/end: Mataram.
Grade of difficulty:Easy/relax.
